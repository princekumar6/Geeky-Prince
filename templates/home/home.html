The economic slowdown ....

June 2, 2020 by Prince

To put simply, a recession is the decline of economic activity, which means that the public have stopped buying products for a while which can cause the downfall of the GDP after a expansion of the economy (A time where products become popular and the income profit of a business becomes large.

Recessions have psychological and confidence aspects. For example, if companies expect economic activity to slow, they may reduce employment levels and save money rather than invest.

Generally an administration gets credit or blame for the state of economy during its time.

Economists usually teach that to some degree recession is unavoidable, and its causes are not well understood.

Consequences

Many economists working within the neoclassical paradigm argue that there is a natural rate of unemployment which, when subtracted from the actual rate of unemployment, can be used to calculate the negative GDP gap during a recession.
